What is this place

More than a farm, a living laboratory

We are a permaculture project in the Atlantic Forest of Espírito Santo. Here, bananas, coffee, açaí and native fruits grow in an agroforestry system since 2021, while we build our structures with tires, earth and ecological bricks.

We're not a resort. We're not experts. We're learners building something real, and you're invited to learn by doing together.
